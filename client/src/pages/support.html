Frequently Asked Questions

What is your response time?
We aim to respond to all queries within 24–48 hours, Monday to Saturday. For urgent issues, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot password?" on the login screen (any login page on our site). Enter your email and we'll send a reset link immediately. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within 5 minutes.
How does KMC upload history work?
When you upload an Excel file in the KMC Data Extraction tool, the file metadata (name, size, date) is saved to your account. You can view all past uploads in your Dashboard under "KMC Uploads". Records are automatically deleted after 30 days.
Is CyManSquare free to use?
Yes — all features are currently free for early adopters. Paid subscription tiers (Basic, Standard, Premium) are being developed and will be introduced in a future update. Existing users will be grandfathered with fair terms.
Can I use CyManSquare on mobile?
The main site and login pages are mobile-friendly. Feature pages (KMC Data Extraction, Project Management) are optimised for desktop use due to the complexity of the tools. Mobile support for features is on our roadmap.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at cymansquare@outlook.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process it within 7 business days.
What file formats does KMC Data Extraction support?
The KMC tool accepts .xlsx, .xls, and .csv files exported from the KMC portal. The file should match the standard KMC data structure for correct parsing.
